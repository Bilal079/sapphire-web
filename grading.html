How Grading Works

Our grading system evaluates products on a scale from 1-10 in four categories:

  • 9-10: Excellent (A+)
  • 8-8.9: Very Good (A)
  • 7-7.9: Good (B+)
  • 6-6.9: Above Average (B)
  • 5-5.9: Average (C+)
  • 4-4.9: Below Average (C)
  • Below 4: Poor (D)
